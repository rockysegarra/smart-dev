Why would you use the URL generator?

What is the function of the URL generator?
The URL generator creates links that let you tailor the product and carting experience to the specific campaigns you are running.

What is Click2Cart?
Click2Cart links let consumers add your products to their carts with a single click.

What is Shopper's Choice?
Shopper’s Choice lets you present the user with options: number of products, preferred retailer, etc, before carting (min 2 clicks).

What is SmartSite?
SmartSite allows to integrated a way of sell products in the own website. With a single click the user send the product to a concret retailer.

What is Catalog?
Catalog lets you send the user to a pre-determined view within the eshop – a product category, set of products, or a single product.

What is Click2Cart?
Click2Cart links let consumers add your products to their carts with a single click

How does it work?
Click2Cart links let consumers add your products to their carts with a single click.

  1. You choose the lead retailer where you want to send the user. You can also activate “related retailers”, to send the user to an alternate retailer(s) if the lead retailer is out of stock.
  2. You determine the lead product or products to be added to the cart, and the quantities to be added. You can also activate “Related products”, which will substitute the closest package size of the same product, if your product is out of stock.
  3. You can add parameters to identify and track your link.
  4. Even after generating the URL, you can return later to modify retailers or products if you want.

What is Shopper's Choice?
Shopper’s Choice lets you present the user with options: number of products, preferred retailer, etc, before carting (min 2 clicks).

How does it work?

  1. You choose which retailer or retailers you would like to offer to your shopper. You can choose one, or as many as you like (we recommend no more than 4).
  2. You determine the lead product or products to be added to the cart, and the default quantities to be added. You can also activate “Related products,” which will substitute the closest package size of the same product, if your product is out of stock.
  3. You can add parameters to identify and track your link.
  4. Even after generating the URL, you can return later to modify retailers or products if you want.

What is SmartSite?
SmartSite can be used on a website, blog, etc. to provide Click2Cart ease for shoppers.

How does it work?

  1. You choose which retailer or retailers you would like to offer to your shopper.
  2. You choose one product and the default quantities to be added. You can activate “Related products”, which will substitute the closest package size of the same product, if your product is out of stock. You can also activate “Product Selector”, which will show a default product size/type but allow the user to chose a different size/type if he prefers.
  3. Copy the code and use it on your website. If you need to modify the code, you can do so in the tool and then re-add it to your site.

What is Catalog?
Catalog lets you send the user to a pre-determined view within the eshop – a product category, set of products, or a single product.

How does it work?

  1. You choose which category, brand, or product to which you would like to send the user.
  2. You can also choose other parameters like retailers or filters.
